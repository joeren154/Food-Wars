FOOD WARS

...Food Wars is a Japanese anime based on the popular manga Shokugeki No Soma about a group of students at culinary school. The main character is Yukihira Soma. The students are pitted against each other in epic cook off battles (food wars) to secure the top places in the school.

Coded by Joeren Rey Guzman and is open-sourced.
